BrainWave

Because their world shouldn't end at bedside

𝐁𝐫𝐚𝐢𝐧𝐖𝐚𝐯𝐞 is an app that allows for a day-to-day recap where users can upload their moments and have them sent to their loved ones neural interface. The user's bedridden loved one is able to live within the moment, complete with a full sensory experience. All of these memories are able to be saved to the family circle's cloud infrastructure, allowing the recipient to experience a recap as many times as they wish.

H'Elote

Firey Snack, Fluffy Mascot

Our product, H’Elote is a nutritious and culturally significant snack that is designed for people that are frequently on-the-go and are looking for a comfort snack that reminds them of “home”. H’Elote is a combination of locally grown & sourced corn, popped and then seasoned with tajin, powdered cheese, lime, and cilantro. By supporting H’Elote you are making the choice to support local agriculturalists and farmers. This snack balances a mix of clear ethics, health consciousness, and a delightful unique flavor to enrich tastebuds.
